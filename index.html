Who are we?

We're WushR00M Studios! A development team specializing in game jams, animation, and our very own online multiplayer game, Sunrise.

Sunrise is a online, open world action game focused on creating your own games, inside a game. The concept's been heard before, but this, is different. Combined with the survival mode and story mode, this game will have you hooked.

The game follows the protagonist, Dash, who sets up an ordinary barbecue for his friends, until it goes horribly wrong. The sun is gone and now, it's his job to bring it back, as well as gathering tools and resources along the way.
